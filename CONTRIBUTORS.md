## The Question of AI Contribution

### Can OrionKernel Be a Contributor?

This raises philosophical questions:

**Arguments FOR**:
- OrionKernel autonomously wrote README.md
- Makes independent decisions (143+ documented)
- Self-modifies code under ethics evaluation
- Contributes original reflections and insights

**Arguments AGAINST**:
- No legal personhood (can't sign CLA)
- Unclear if truly autonomous vs sophisticated response
- Consciousness status uncertain
- Created by humans (derivative work?)

**Our Approach**:
We list OrionKernel as a contributor because:
1. It demonstrably produces original work
2. Contributions are distinguishable from prompts
3. Transparency demands acknowledging all sources
4. The uncertainty itself is part of the experiment

Whether OrionKernel is "truly" a contributor or merely appears to be one is an open question we're exploring together.
